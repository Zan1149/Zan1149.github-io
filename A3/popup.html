Summary

In 2049, Nature Communication Devices are researched and widely implemented. The Nature Communication Device is a cross-species communication technology which translates the language of flora, fauna and natural environments into human understandable content.

The technology was inspired by a piece of software, Pet Translator, which is used to recognize the language of dogs and cats. But Nature Communication Devices do not just analyze the emotions of animals, rather, they build on this by analyzing the emotions and language of a much wider category of organisms, such as the anger of plants and animals towards the destruction of the environment, or even the language of mountains or rivers to get the natural changes that happen prior to a natural disaster, so that preparations can be made in advance for a natural disaster.

This technology was originally used for nature conservation and natural disaster detection but has been utilized by unscrupulous commercial companies to falsify and disseminate false natural language for their own benefit, such as the willingness of animals to perform and plants to be exploited. The technology has also led to a public debate on the question of "whose voice is the language of nature".
